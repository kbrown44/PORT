ABOUT KYLE

Kyle is a Digital Artist specializing in Photography, Video, and Design. Since graduating Summa Cum Laude from Stony Brook University with a BA in Studio Art, Kyle has taken on various projects in both the Commercial and Fine Art sectors. His aesthetic follows an abstract minimalist ideal that combines his Fine Art teaching with his hands-on technical training. Kyle has been awarded honors such as the Leon Bolotin Scholarship Award and was twice a finalist in Photographer’s Forum Magazine’s “Best of Photography” competitions. Aside from creating, Kyle’s passions include football, comics, films, and fitness.
